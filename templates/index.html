Welcome


A huge number of developmentally dynamic genes (DDGs) lead to phenotypic differences across developmental stages. Especially, developmentally dynamic lncRNAs have been found to enrich for functionally relevant lncRNAs, and thus useful for the identification of potential functional lncRNAs.


DevoGene, a comprehensive database of DDGs, systematically integrated all public developmental transcriptomic datasets and related publications with more than two developmental stages, and identified 434,132 DDGs in 24 species by curating high-throughput datasets of 2,360 samples from 6,850 experiments of 67 projects.
